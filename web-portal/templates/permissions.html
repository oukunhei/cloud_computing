Security Design Notes
  • Developer cannot access secrets to prevent credential leakage if their kubeconfig is compromised.
  • Developer cannot modify RBAC, quotas, or network policies — these are platform-level controls managed by admins only.
  • Viewer cannot execute commands in pods or port-forward, preventing unauthorized access to running applications.
  • NetworkPolicy enforces isolation at the network layer, independent of RBAC.
  • ResourceQuota prevents noisy-neighbor problems by capping total resource consumption per namespace.
  • LimitRange ensures every pod has sensible defaults, preventing accidental resource exhaustion.
